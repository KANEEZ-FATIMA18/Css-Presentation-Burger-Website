about us

our story

It all began with a passion for flavor and a dream to create more than just food—an experience. From our humble beginnings as a food truck serving burgers with heart, to becoming a local hotspot with a speakeasy twist, our journey has been all about bringing people together over great food and unforgettable moments. Every step we’ve taken is fueled by a love for craft, creativity, and community.

1994

The Journey Begins

Our story started on four wheels and a dream. With a food truck and a passion for crafting unforgettable burgers, we brought bold flavors to the streets, creating a loyal following that craved every bite.

1994

The first restaurant

Seven years later, we traded our wheels for a cozy space where people could come together to enjoy our signature creations. Opening our first restaurant was the beginning of a new chapter.

1994

The speakeasy

We took things up a notch by opening a hidden gem—a speakeasy that blended mystery, craft cocktails, and our signature style. This secret spot became the perfect place to unwind, connect, and enjoy.

1994

Cheers to the Top 50

After years of dedication, we reached an incredible milestone: being recognized as one of the Top 50 Cocktail Bars. This honor reflects our commitment to creativity and quality.

MEET THE TEAM

We started with a food truck and a dream: to bring real, unapologetic flavor to burger lovers. No shortcuts, no compromises—just good food, great vibes, and even better company.
